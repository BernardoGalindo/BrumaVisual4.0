Who I Am

My story is one of two worlds colliding: the rugged road of a 4000km motorcycle journey across South America and the precise, data-driven world of a biotechnological engineer. For over six years, I've managed complex environmental projects in the demanding landscapes of the Ecuadorian Amazon. This wasn't just a job; it was a proving ground. It taught me to see the world with two sets of eyes: the adventurer's, seeking the story and the feeling of a place, and the engineer's, demanding accuracy and technical rigor in every detail.

My Mission

Bruma Visual was born from that intersection. My mission is to bring the world's most remote and beautiful places to life, not just through pretty pictures, but through immersive, data-rich experiences. I use advanced drone, photogrammetry, and 360° photography to capture the very essence of a location—the feeling of being there. For businesses and individuals, this means offering a way to showcase their projects, properties, or adventures with breathtaking detail and undeniable accuracy.

My Work

From creating detailed 3D models of complex terrain to building immersive virtual tours, my work is about providing a new perspective. The skills I've honed in environmental supervision—strategic thinking, problem-solving, and a relentless focus on quality—are the foundation of every project I undertake. I don't just deliver visuals; I deliver reliable, actionable insights wrapped in a compelling story.
